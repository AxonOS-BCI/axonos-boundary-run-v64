How to play

  • Hold the boundary. Steer your signal through the field. Survive as integrity drains under pressure.
  • Raw-signal (red, −18) and artifacts (violet, −7) cost integrity — dodge them, or slip past closely for a near-miss bonus.
  • Vaults (gold) restore integrity and build a combo streak.
  • Dash (Shift) gives a burst with brief invulnerability — pass through a threat to purge it and keep your combo. It has a cooldown.
  • Audit band restores integrity; Fast band doubles score but drains it. Risk for reward.
  • Consent gates suspend scoring briefly — a setback to avoid.

The replay proof

Every run is a deterministic simulation: the same seed and input log reproduce the exact same outcome and a single canonical SHA-256 hash. Export proof saves a small JSON you — or anyone — can re-verify offline with the included Python tool, in a different language, with no backend involved.

Zero telemetry · offline · no backend. An AxonOS Foundation Standard educational artifact — not a medical or BCI device.
