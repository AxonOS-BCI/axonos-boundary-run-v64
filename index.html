How to play

  • Move: Arrows or WASD (A/D, W/S) — free 2-D movement, no gravity.
  • Dash: Shift or K — a burst with brief invulnerability. Dash through a red or purple threat to purge it for bonus points (your combo survives). On a cooldown.
  • Dodge: red Raw-Signal (−18) and purple Artifacts (−7). Slip past closely for a near-miss bonus.
  • Collect: gold Vaults for integrity, score and combo streaks.
  • Bands: green Audit restores integrity; amber Fast doubles score but drains it.
  • Touch: drag to steer; two-finger tap to dash. P pauses; Enter restarts.
